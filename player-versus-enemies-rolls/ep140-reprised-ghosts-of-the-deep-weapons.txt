The Restoration Ritual origin trait provides you with an automatic magazine refill from reserves after reviving a teammate or finishing an enemy which can be handy, but is probably less useful on a primary, while Gravity Well can be very useful to pick up ammo bricks within 5m on a manual reload. Recommended MW: Stability, with a Backup mod or, when masterworked, Synergy to create Firesprites or, when the artifact mod's around, Stunloader.
