// Zealous Ideal - PvE Minor god 1
// (Fluted Barrel, Arrowhead Brake, Corkscrew Rifling), (Ionized Heatsink, Enhanced Heatsink, Accelerated Heatsink, Light Heatsink, Tactical Heatsink), (Attrition Orbs), (Burning Ambition)
//notes:Inspired by @CourtProjects, @Saint_Kabr and and @ImpetusAlways (PvE PvE-MinorSpec PvE-EndGame PvE-God M+KB Controller): First-choice PvE roll for single-target damage against tougher enemies in both general at-level or better, and end-game underleveled content, particularly for solar builds. Zealous Ideal is a Balanced Heat frame auto rifle, with a precision firing rate, which are rated C-Tier for endgame PvE by @TheAegisRelic tiering spreadsheet https://linktr.ee/TheAegisRelic being similar in performance to other autos. The heat frame is like an extra perk set, first providing reduced heat generation, significantly increasing the number of shots before overheating, and providing a pseudo-Reconstruction, automatically "refilling" as the weapon loses heat when not being used. Additionally, there's an intrinsic ×1.15 damage boost against orange and yellow bars and vehicles, plus a ×2 vent rate if you manually reload when the heat bar is at >87.5% of maximum heat to proc Perfect Vent, as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. The stats are good by comparison with other precision autos - see https://d2foundry.gg/w/1863583117 for how the weapon stats impact in-game performance, once the formulas have been worked out. With the effect of the precision intrinsic reducing horizonal yaw to ×0.8 you can ignore Recoil Direction and focus on Handling with the barrel perk. For the heatsink perk, look for something that reduces heat generation, or improves cooling efficiency or vent speed. For an auto to use in underleveled content, Attrition Orbs spawns a power orb after 2/3 mag +2 hits within 5s of each other to proc all your useful armour mods. Burning Ambition works to build ignitions on tougher targets with a solar ability to apply scorch initially, otherwise it'll take 55% of the magazine to apply +10 scorch stacks with +10 added on each additional hit to build towards an ignition at 100. All of the scorch stacks are increased by ×1.5 with Ember of Ashes. The Origin Trait, Imperial Allegiance grants a stack for each ally in 15m, up to ×3, to boost both the cooling rate and damage when heat is >50% by up to a max of ×1.105, so is ideal for working in a fireteam. If you're interested, this is modelled on the E-11 medium blaster rifle, the standard-issue weapon for the Galactic Empire's stormtroopers. Recommended MW: Cooling Efficiency, with a Heat Generated mod or your preferred choice of mod to boost weapon stats, ammo generation, or elemental pickups for builds - and don't forget to check any available seasonal fragile mods.|tags:PvE PvE-MinorSpec PvE-EndGame PvE-God M+KB Controller
