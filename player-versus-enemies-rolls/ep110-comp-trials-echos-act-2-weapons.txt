The Riposte is the Final Shape expansion's Competitive playlist reward - a unique Lightweight frame AR which grants the usual +20 Mobility and +6.25% movement speed with the fire rate of a rapid-fire. Aim to get to around 80 Stability, and you should be fine. If control isn't so much of an issue for you, then Short Action Stock or Composite Stock will boost Handling for a snappier gunfeel. For add-clearing, the void trait perk pairing of Repulsor Brace and Destabilizing Rounds work really well together, and synergise with builds using void aspects and fragments. Recommended MW: Handling or Reload Speed, with a Backup Mag mod.
