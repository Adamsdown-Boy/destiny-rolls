//notes:Inspired by @PodvsEnemies episode 139 from 13 May 2025 (https://open.spotify.com/episode/0m6Sfdv88fNZmYm9A6PdVR?si=140d643338154357) with @ImpetusAlways @CourtProjects and @Saint_Kabr (PvE PvE-MinorSpec PvE-God PvE-EndGame M+KB Controller): First-choice PvE pick for clearing Minor adds in both general at-level or better, and end-game underleveled content, particularly with an arc build. Handling is the main weakness, so look to improve that from the barrel perk to make things snappier. For the magazine perk, either choose your preferred combat effect or look to speed up the reload a bit. For arc builds there's a combination of Eddy Current to speed up reload further, together with Rolling Storm to build up stacks of Bolt Charge for single target damage plus a bit of AoE. But, as an alternative first trait column perk, Dragonfly provides an AoE arc elemental explosion on a crit kill for double damage perks, which are always tough to pass on. For the origin trait you have Vanguard's Vindication for 3HP healing per kill, Stunning Recovery for 60HP and +40 recovery for 3s when stunning a champion, or Omolon Fluid Dynamics for better stability and reload speed in the top half of the magazine for compulsive reloaders.
