Scintillation (that is, the process of being sparkly) is the first Strand adaptive burst LFR. It's got decent stats and feels good to use, and the Veist Stinger, while not the powerhouse it once more, is still the best origin trait to have on an LFR. With this being a three shot burst, you'll imp[rove control with Arrowhead Brake as the barrel perk. The best battery perk is Enhanced Battery which, with a Backup Mag mod, is your first step towards the maximum 7 shots but, if you get Accelerated Coils that also works to improve DPS rate at the cost of a small drop to total damage. In the first trait column, Envious Assassin can triple your number of shots to 21 - pretty much all of your reserve ammo. In the second, Reservoir Burst is the final step to get to 7 shots in the base battery, adds +25% damage to every overflowed shot and, if you kill your target, the explosion is 4x stronger than Chain Reaction would be. Recommended MW: Handling or Charge Time, with a Backup Mag mod.
