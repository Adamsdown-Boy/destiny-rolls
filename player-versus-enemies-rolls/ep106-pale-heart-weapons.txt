First-choice PvE pick for add-clearing Minor Spec enemies and fireteam support in end-game content, particularly with solar/prismatic fragments that boost healing. A new Support frame auto-rifle subtype that builds healing charges on enemy hits (there;s a semi-circle below the reticule that fills up) which you can then hip fire at allies to heal them. Hitting six healing shots grants the weapon a 10% damage boost for 5s and cures you and nearby allies. No Hesitation is probably the strongest solar primary currently in the game. It's a bit weird to fire as it's not hitscan - but if you can get used to Osteo Striga's tracking shots, you'll do fine. First off, controller players might benefit from a bit of extra stability from the barrel and mag perks. The first and second trait column perks can double down on the frame intrinsic perks through Physic which grants x1 Restoration (x2 when enhanced) for 5s to you and allies, and Circle of Life which boosts damage on healing by an additional +25% (for +37.5% total) for 10s for both you and the healed Guardian. The Dealer's Choice origin trait is nice to have (who doesn't love more super energy?) - with two or three Pale Heart weapons, you'll notice the difference! Recommended MW: Stability, with a Backup Mag mod. Tags: PvE, PvE-MinorSpec, PvE-Utility, PvE-EndGame, PvE-God, M+KB, Controller

// Embraced Identity (First-choice PvE)
//notes:Inspired by @DestinyMBP Podcast vs Enemies episode 106 from 18 June 2024 (https://open.spotify.com/episode/0GYlriUxhFqNHGLqb84C1G?si=bb5a2c9b339d4c35) with @ImpetusAlways, @CourtProjects and @Saint_Kabr. First-choice PvE pick for burst damage against major and bosses in end-game content. If you already have a good backup DPS sniper then you can safely pass on Embraced Identity. Decent base stats so, as ever, handling is the stat to boost with the barrel perk. Tactical Mag plus a Backup Mag mod gets you to 6 shots, or Appended Mag + Backup Mag gets you to 7. In the first trait column, Rewind Rounds or Reconstruction are both excellent.
