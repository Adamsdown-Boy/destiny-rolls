You can use pretty much any barrel perk to boost your preferred stat that doesn't apply a malus. In the magazine perk slot, look for an option to boost Reload Speed. Within the useful range of SMGs, CQC trait perks shine - in the first column, Proximity Power stacks up ×3 times to provide +15 (+18 when enhanced) to your Melee attribute per stack on kills when another enemy's within 15m - a total a +45 (+54) for a refreshable 10s on further kills, so you can invest stat points into other armour attributes. The Vanguard Vindication Origin Trait provides up to 3 stacks of ×0.98/0.96/0.94 damage reduction for 10s, for kills within 20s of using a class ability. There's also the fun Wild Card for the chance of Telesto-like "Experimental Submunitions" dropping on a kill - the number depends on the enemy tier - that last up to 10.5s and explode on contact or proximity to an enemy.
