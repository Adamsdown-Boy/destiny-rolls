// Monody-44 - PvP Kill-Chain god 1
// (Hammer-Forged Rifling), (Projection Fuse), (Discord), (Closing Time)
//notes:Inspired by https://www.youtube.com/@Legoleflash (PvP PvP-KillChain PvP-God M+KB Controller): God-roll PvP for kill-chaining in 6s. Monody-44 is, at time of writing, a solid A-Tier or high-end B-Tier special option, which Lego describes as having "an accuracy cone that ignores the law of physics" and is demonstrated in Lego's video. The @hugowo_ spreadsheet at https://www.destiny2.science/, shows a base charge time for high-impact fusions of 967ms - the v9.5.0 patch nerfed the charge time slightly from .960s - and a 3-bolt kill, out of 5 bolts fired. High Impacts reward a play slower and methodical approach from the huge frame intrinsic of an ×0.8 accuracy cone size and ×0.83 accuracy cone growth when stationary or moving slowly (<2.8m/s which is walking speed when crouched). That's the equivalent of the average Under Pressure cone size buff and the original unnerfed Firmly Planted accuracy cone growth buff rolled into one. The stats aren't great compared to the high-impact king Glacioclasm, but as the only one that offers tiered rolls, you can get enhanced barrel, mag, weapon mods and origin traits to fill all of the stat gaps at Tiers 4 and 5. You can see the in-game effect of stat changes at https://d2foundry.gg/w/3201200906 for yourself. Range with the barrel and battery perks is key to controlling the recoil - ideally without any stat penalties. In the first trait perk column for kill-chaining, Discord grants a +30 Aerial Effectiveness, ×0.75 ADS animation modifier and a ×0.75 accuracy cone size for 7s (8s when enhanced) after swapping within 5s of another weapon kill, but crucially any kills while active refund a shot from thin air so you'll not run out of ammo. In the second column Closing Time provides a linearly scaling buff from half full battery to empty up to +10 Range, +25 Handling, ×0.95 accuracy cone size and a ×0.90 handling animation scalar at max effect. The Suros Synergy origin trait is useful for PvP - on finishing a reload, you'll gain +40 Handling and ×0.8 flinch for 6s. Recommended MW: Charge Time, with a Range enhanced mod, or your choice depending on your play style.|tags:PvP PvP-KillChain PvP-God M+KB Controller
