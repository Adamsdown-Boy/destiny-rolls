//notes:Inspired by https://www.youtube.com/@Legoleflash (PvP PvP-Duel PvP-God M+KB Controller): God-roll PvP for duelling. The Modified B-7 Pistol is, at time of writing, a solid A-Tier or high-end B-Tier primary, which Lego describes as having been seen 3-tapping in the hands of every streamer since the launch of Renegades, demonstrated in his video (https://youtu.be/Oc6bHfJCR3w). Heat frames are like having an extra perk set, first providing a pseudo-Reconstruction, automatically "refilling" as the weapon loses heat when not being used. Additionally, Dynamic Heat weapons have an intrinsic ×1.025 damage boost against Guardians in Crucible, plus a ×0.5 heat generation for 6s if you manually reload when the heat bar is at <12.5% of maximum heat to proc Dynamic Heat Limiter, as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. In addition, this also has the precision frame intrinsic of a ×0.8 horizontal recoil, so you can pretty much ignore the Recoil Direction stat. The @hugowo_ spreadsheet at https://www.destiny2.science/, shows a base TTK of 1.000s (2c2b). The stats aren't great compared to other precision hand cannons, but you can get enhanced barrel, mag, weapon mods and origin traits to fill all of the stat gaps at Tiers 4 and 5. You can see the in-game effect of stat changes at https://d2foundry.gg/w/3146657388 for yourself. Look for a barrel perk that boosts Range, preferably without any penalties. As the heatsink perk, Overclocked Heatsink boosts damage by ×1.02 at the cost of -10 Cooling Efficiency - but this is enough for a 3-tap in 0.667s (3c 0b) with 178 weapons stat, without needing any damage-boosting trait perks. To make the most of this 3-tap roll, start in the first trait perk column with Delicate Tuning, which works like a Heat weapon's mirror to Closing Time, boosting Range by +20 (+22 when enhanced), Handling and Accuracy when a weapon's cold, with the benefits reducing to half at 50% heat. As a pairing in the second column there's Opening Shot, for +25 Range, +20 Aim Assist (+30 and +25 when enhanced), ×0.95 accuracy cone size and ×0.90 accuracy cone growth on your first shot, with a 3s cooldown. Alternatively, Eye of the Storm grants a linearly scaling buff from 40 shield HP to 0 HP (critical health), maxxing out at +30 Handing, ×0.6 accuracy cone size and ×0.83 accuracy cone growth (+33, ×0.56 and ×0.81 enhanced) until your shield regenerates to 80HP - this should start kicking in as you lose Delicate Tuning's boosts. Finally, there's Headstone to generate a Stasis crystal on a crit kill which stops revives, and can be shattered for AoE damage and to proc stasis ability effects. The Fleet Footed origin trait is useful for PvP, stacking up to ×1 on hits and ×2 on kills to boost movement speed and Airborne Effectiveness for a refreshable 5s (6s enhanced). Accelerated Assault sounds good, providing 10% cooling and ×1.05 damage when you hit 3 times with 0.8-1.2s between each hit, but it takes at least 1.6s to proc, so you're not likely to see any benefit within a normal TTK window. Recommended MW: Range, with a Range enhanced mod, or your choice depending on your play style.|tags:PvP PvP-Duel PvP-God M+KB Controller
