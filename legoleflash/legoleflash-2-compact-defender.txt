//notes:Inspired by https://www.youtube.com/@Legoleflash (PvP PvP-Duel PvP-God M+KB Controller): God-roll PvP for duelling in 3s and 6s. Compact Defender is, at time of writing, an A-Tier option for skilled players who can manage the Dynamic Heat frame and probably a B-Tier for the rest of us. "A sidearm that makes your first fight easier and still lets you 4-tap afterwards" according to Lego. Heat frames are like having an extra perk set, first providing a pseudo-Reconstruction, automatically "refilling" as the weapon loses heat when not being used. Additionally, Dynamic Heat weapons have an intrinsic ×1.025 damage boost against Guardians in Crucible, plus a ×0.5 heat generation for 6s if you manually reload when the heat bar is at <12.5% of maximum heat to proc Dynamic Heat Limiter, as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. On top of that you also get the Lightweight frame boosts of +20 to Mobility and ×1.0625 base movement speed. The @hugowo_ spreadsheet at https://www.destiny2.science/, shows a base TTK of 0.667s (4c 1b). The stats are a bit mixed when comparing to other lightweights - good stability and ammo generation, but bad recoil direction, but as the only one that offers tiered rolls, you can get enhanced barrel, mag, weapon mods and origin traits to help out at Tiers 4 and 5. You can see the in-game effect of stat changes at https://d2foundry.gg/w/2819552809 for yourself. Range is highly compressed on sidearms so, for a controllable PvP roll aim for a total Range of around 43 and Stability of 70 with your barrel and heatsink perks, Masterwork and mod. While Rangefinder might seem the obvious pick, Delicate Tuning works like a Heat weapon's mirror to Closing Time, boosting Range by +20 (+22 when enhanced), Handling and Accuracy when a weapon's cold, with the benefits reducing to half at 50% heat. Enhanced Precision Instrument boosts Precision Instrument's crit damage by ×1.2 which means a very forgiving 0.667s (3c 2b) - but if you can run a weapon stat of 164 and two Void surge mods, picking up an orb after a kill will reduce the TTK to 0.5s (4c 0b) - the same as Kill Clip, but without having to get a kill first. You can see the play loop demonstrated in Lego's video. The Air-Cooled Core can be useful in PvP - when at <10 heat you gain Handling and improved ADS speed for the start of a duel, and after sprinting for 0.5s you'll gain improved vent speed. Accelerated Assault sounds good, providing 10% cooling and ×1.05 damage when you hit 3 times with 0.8-1.2s between each hit, but it takes at least 1.6s to proc, so you're not likely to see any benefit within a normal TTK window. Recommended MW: Stability, Range or Ballistics, with a Range or Stability enhanced mod, or your choice depending on your play style.|tags:PvP PvP-Duel PvP-God M+KB Controller
