//notes:Inspired by https://www.youtube.com/@Legoleflash (PvP PvP-Duel PvP-God M+KB Controller): God-roll PvP for duelling. The M-17 "Fast Talker" is, at time of writing, an S-Tier primary, which Lego describes as "an ultra-accurate bullet hose in PvP", demonstrated in his video (https://youtu.be/LldDti54Gwo?si=9j382iCFlMJE7tuF). The heat frame is like an extra perk set, first providing reduced heat generation, significantly increasing the number of shots before overheating, and providing a pseudo-Reconstruction, automatically "refilling" as the weapon loses heat when not being used. Balanced Heat weapons have a ×2 vent rate if you manually reload when the heat bar is at >87.5% of maximum heat to proc Perfect Vent, as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. Additionally, this offers the +20 Mobility stat boost and ×1.0625 base movement speed of a Lightweight frame. The @hugowo_ spreadsheet at https://www.destiny2.science/, shows a base TTK of 0.733s (11c1b) - this is made easier (10c2b) at 175 weapons state, and a faster 0.667s TTK (11c0b) at 186 weapons. The 80% crit accuracy-adjusted TTK is 0.800s (10c3b), made easier (9c4b) at 164 weapons. The stats package is one of the best looking for lightweight SMGs, and you can get enhanced barrel, mag, weapon mods and origin traits to improve further at Tiers 4 and 5. You can see the in-game effect of stat changes at https://d2foundry.gg/w/1419158093 for yourself. For this duelling god roll, look for all range with barrel and mag perks. Then to boost range further, in the first trait perk column there's Delicate Tuning, which works like a Heat weapon's mirror to Closing Time, boosting Range by +20 (+22 when enhanced), Handling and Accuracy when a weapon's cold, with the benefits reducing to half at 50% heat - so your first shots will be at or near 100 range. As a pairing in the second column we can use Tap the Trigger, which for 0.6s (0.76s when enhanced) after a trigger pull provides +40 Stability, ×0.5 recoil and ×0.9 Accuracy Cone size. With a high enough weapons stat, this buff should last as long as your TTK, combining 100 Range, 89 Stability, halved recoil and the Lightweight frame boosts. You can see the gunplay for this in Lego's video. The Fleet Footed origin trait is useful for PvP, stacking up to ×1 on hits and ×2 on kills to boost movement speed and Airborne Effectiveness for a refreshable 5s (6s enhanced). Accelerated Assault sounds good, providing 10% cooling and ×1.05 damage when you hit 3 times with 0.8-1.2s between each hit, but it takes at least 1.6s to proc, so you're not likely to see any benefit within a normal TTK window. Recommended MW: Range, with a Range mod.|tags:PvP PvP-Duel PvP-God M+KB Controller

The M-17 "Fast Talker" is a Balanced Heat SMG which are rated C-Tier for endgame PvE by @TheAegisRelic tiering spreadsheet https://linktr.ee/TheAegisRelic, and which Lego describes as "a machine gun disguised as an SMG", demonstrated in his video (https://youtu.be/LldDti54Gwo?si=9j382iCFlMJE7tuF). The heat frame is like an extra perk set, first providing reduced heat generation, significantly increasing the number of shots before overheating, and providing a pseudo-Reconstruction, automatically "refilling" as the weapon loses heat when not being used. Balanced Heat weapons have a ×2 vent rate if you manually reload when the heat bar is at >87.5% of maximum heat to proc Perfect Vent, as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. Additionally, this offers the +20 Mobility stat boost and ×1.0625 base movement speed of a Lightweight frame. The stats package is one of the best looking for lightweight SMGs, and you can get enhanced barrel, mag, weapon mods and origin traits to improve further at Tiers 4 and 5. You can see the in-game effect of stat changes at https://d2foundry.gg/w/2770035786 for yourself. Look for some extra range with the barrel perk, without any penalties. Then, to maximise firing time, look for a heatsink perk that reduces heat generation. In the first trait perk column, Attrition Orbs spawns a power orb after 20-22 hits, depending on the heatsink perk, within 5s of each other to proc armour mods, while in the second Target Lock stacks up to 5 times on repeated hits within 0.2s of each other for up to ×1.4 damage at 110.33% of base magazine capacity - so should proc if you can time your manual vent correctly. This, particularly with Titan Peacekeepers, should be a good primary to use in longer boss DPS phases to generate super energy, especially if you get the rare Accelerated Assault origin trait. For the Origin Trait Air-Cooled Core improves handling and ADS speed when heat is <10%, and boosts Vent Speed for 6s after sprinting. You can see the gunplay for this in Lego's video. The Fleet Footed origin trait is probably more useful for PvP, stacking up to ×1 on hits and ×2 on kills to boost movement speed and Airborne Effectiveness for a refreshable 5s (6s enhanced). If you're lucky, you'll drop one with the rare Accelerated Assault origin trait which provides 10% cooling and ×1.05 damage when you hit 3 times with 0.8-1.2s between each hit.
