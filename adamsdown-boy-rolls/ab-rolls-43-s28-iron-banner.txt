Kills on any affected enemy will proc Repulsor Brace's ×0.5 damage resisting Void overshield for 8s (10s enhanced) after killing a void-debuffed enemy - duration boosted by ×1.5 with Echo of Persistence. The only origin trait on offer is Skulking Wolf which, after you get a kill at ≤90 HP, enhances radar and makes adds less accurate when firing at you for 10s. If you're interested, Bracus Reghusk was one of the Cabal deployed to the Scarlet Keep by Empress Caiatl to support the Guardians in stopping the Hive invasion of the fortress in PsiOps Battleground: Moon.
