// Triple Laureate - PvE Minor god 1
// (Barrel Shroud, Corkscrew Rifling, Smallbore), (Tactical Mag, Appended Mag, Extended Mag, Alloy Mag, Flared Magwell), (Slideshot, Grave Robber), (Chaos Reshaped)
//notes:Adamsdown_Boy written as at v9.5.6 (PvE PvE-Minor PvE-SingleAdd PvE-EndGame PvE-God M+KB Controller): First-choice PvE roll for Minor add clearing in both general at-level or better, and end-game underleveled content. Triple Laureate is a spread shot hand cannon, which are rated B-Tier for endgame PvE by @TheAegisRelic tiering spreadsheet https://linktr.ee/TheAegisRelic being a huge statistical outlier but a little niche and close-range. The frame intrinsic fires 12 pellets with a max 25m range, after which they disappear as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. It's difficult to make direct comparisons with the D2Foundary website down but hopefully when it's back https://d2foundry.gg/w/1605599021 will show how the weapon stats impact in-game performance. With the constrained range, focus on Handling with the barrel perk to make it snappier, or maybe a little extra stability. With the magazine perk, focus on extra shots or faster reload. For end-game use, for reloads, in the first trait perk column Slideshot refills 15% (rounded up) of the mag when you slide, and boosts both Range and Stability by +20 (+22 when enhanced) for 2.5s or on the first shot fired. If you're willing to get closer, Grave Robber refills your magazine on an elemental melee hit or any melee kill. In the second column, for damage, Chaos Reshaped stacks up to ×2 on dealing or recoving damage within a 5s window for at least 12s (11.5s enhanced) - at ×1 it provides a ×1.2 damage boost for a refreshable 7s and then at ×2 provides ×1.35 damage for a refreshable 7s together with a healing burst of up to 35HP that also starts health regeneration. There's the choice of Classy Contender origin trait which provides 5% (6% enhanced) Class ability energy on kills, which is useful if not game-changing, or Hakke Breach Armaments passively boosts damage against vehicles by ×1.15 (×1.2 enhanced), turrets and constructs by ×1.3 (×1.6 enhanced) and Stasis crystals by ×1.45. Recommended MW: Handling, with your preferred choice of mod to boost weapon stats, ammo generation, or elemental pickups for builds - and don't forget to check any available seasonal fragile mods.|tags:PvE PvE-Minor PvE-SingleAdd PvE-EndGame PvE-God M+KB Controller

//notes:Adamsdown_Boy written as at v9.5.6 (PvE PvE-Boss PvE-SustainedDamage PvE-God PvE-EndGame M+KB Controller): First-choice PvE roll for sustained boss DPS in both general at-level or better, and end-game underleveled content. Keraunios is an adaptive trace rifle, which are rated B-Tier for endgame PvE by @TheAegisRelic tiering spreadsheet https://linktr.ee/TheAegisRelic being useful for shooting objectives and keeping special, with a decent add clearing score as well. The frame intrinsic doesn't offer any benefits beyond well-rounded stats as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. It's difficult to make direct comparisons with the D2Foundary website down but hopefully when it's back https://d2foundry.gg/w/981450701 will show how the weapon stats impact in-game performance. The Recoil Direction is a perfect 100, so on Controller look for Stability to help keep hitting crits with the barrel perk, and on M+KB maybe boost Handling. For the battery perk, you can look to improve the battery's capacity. For sustained boss DPS, Fourth Time's the Charm generates 2 shots from thin air after 4 crits within 2s (3s when enhanced) of each other, so is top choice. The other refill perks can work - Rewind Rounds will delay reloading or, if you have time to set up, Overflow provides for a double capacity battery before you start. If you're running an Arc subclass and can become Amplified or generate Bolt Charge, there's also Supercharged Magazine or Trickle Charge. For damage, Target Lock stacks up to 5 times on repeated hits within 0.2s of each other for up to ×1.4 damage at 110.33% of base magazine capacity - so works best on those overflowed or auto-refilled magazines. The Classy Contender origin trait provides 5% (6% enhanced) Class ability energy on kills, which is useful if not game-changing. If you're interested,the name is derived from the ancient Greek word "κεραυνός" - a lightning bolt or thunderbolt.
