//notes:Adamsdown_Boy written as at v9.1.5.3 (PvE PvE-MinorSpec PvE-God PvE-EndGame M+KB Controller): First-choice PvE roll for tougher add control in both general at-level or better, and end-game underleveled content, particularly on melee builds. For the Origin Trait, there's either Veist Stinger which provides a 2% chance per hit to refill 25% of the magazine or Search Party which applies a ×0.85 ADS animation scalar and ×1.1 ADS movement speed when there are no allies within 15m. You might also consider Kinetic Tremors against tougher enemies which emits three 6m AoE kinetic shockwaves after 14 hits (13 when enhanced) within 3s of each other, which are treated as weapon damage, inheriting damage boosts and also proccing weapon effects like Bewildering Burst. For the Origin Trait, there's either Veist Stinger which provides a 2% chance per hit to refill 25% of the magazine or Search Party which applies a ×0.85 ADS animation scalar and ×1.1 ADS movement speed when there are no allies within 15m. Arcane Embrace was the first heavy burst frame shotgun and are rated C-Tier for endgame PvE by @TheAegisRelic tiering spreadsheet https://linktr.ee/TheAegisRelic. While they struggle to find a defined role in the current sandbox, the subfamily does offer good single mag damage. The stats are decent by comparison with its peers, and are helped further at Tier 4/5 when barrel and magazine perks are enhanced - see https://d2foundry.gg/w/3328019216 for how the weapon stats impact in-game performance. From the @Mossy-Max spreadsheet at https://www.destiny2.science/ base Magazine Size is 12 (max 16) - each trigger pull fires two shots - and total ammo is 36. The usual play for shotguns of boosting Handling with the barrel perk still applies and, as there's no Assault Mag, look to a magazine perk that helps with Reload Speed. For burst damage, you have a wide range of decent first column trait perks to bypass manual reloads, boost Reload Speed, or overflow the magazine to choose from. You'll likely be in CQC ranges, so Surrounded procs to boost damage by up to ×1.4 (×1.47 when enhanced) while within 15m of 3+ enemies. For the Origin Trait, there's only Search Party which applies a ×0.85 ADS animation scalar and ×1.1 ADS movement speed when there are no allies within 15m.
