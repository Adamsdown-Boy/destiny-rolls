// Tinasha's Mastery (PvE)
// (Quick Launch, Hard Launch, Smart Drift Control, Linear Compensator), (High-Velocity Rounds, Implosion Rounds), Deconstruct, Chill Clip
//notes:Adamsdown_Boy written v8.1.5.1 (PvE PvE-MinorSpec PvE-MajorSpec PvE-EndGame PvE-God M+KB Controller): PvE pick use against bulky Minor and Major enemies in general, and end-game underleveled content. Probably the most chased-after Iron Banner PvE weapon in years, Tinasha's Mastery is the first Stasis rocket-assisted sidearm. At base the stats are solid - 28 Range equates to 14.56m when ADS and 12.12m hipfire (although there really isn't that much damage dropoff), 42 Handling is 0.31s ready, 0.25s ADS and 0.35s stow speed, while the Reload Speed of 29 is a 1.81s reload. Magazine size is 10 (maximum 16), and with 0/1/2/3 reserves mods you get total ammo of 40/43/46/48 (max 57). This roll is all about slowing and freezing single targets, so Velocity is far more important to boost than Blast Radius with the barrel and magazine perks. At the time of writing, Deconstruct might be bugged, with both the impact and explosive damage counting towards proccing, after which you get 2 shots returned (10% of the magazine, as long as it's 11 or more) for every 2 shots fired, as well as the bonus damage to constructs and vehicles. All of this is building up to Chill Clip in the second trait perk column - one shot slows, and disrupts, Overload Champions and three shots freezes, and stuns, Unstoppable champions, which you can synergise with Stasis fragments. Apply Radiant and it'll pierce Barrier champions too. The Deconstruct/Chill Clip combo keeps your magazine above half full which means you can shoot an entire magazine's worth of slowing and freezing without reloads. It's bound to be nerfed at some point, so enjoy being totally overpowered for as long as it lasts. With the change to the Skulking Wolf origin trait, at least there is now an effect in PvE to reduce enemy accuracy when it's procced, which could be useful for survivability in more difficult content. Recommended MW: Velocity with a Backup Mag mod or, when enhanced, a Synergy mod to generate Stasis shards or Stunloader when an artifact mod's around.|tags:PvE PvE-MinorSpec PvE-MajorSpec PvE-EndGame PvE-God M+KB Controller
