// Sarpedon-D - PvE Minor god AB 1 (god-pve)
// (Barrel Shroud, Full Choke, Corkscrew Rifling, Smallbore), (Tactical Mag, Alloy Mag, Flared Magwell, Appended Mag), (Eddy Current), (Voltshot)
//notes:Adamsdown_Boy written as at v9.5.6.2 (PvE PvE-Minor PvE-SingleAdd PvE-God PvE-EndGame M+KB Controller): First-choice PvE roll for clearing individual Minor adds or tougher enemies in activities rated Normal, Hard or Very Hard difficulty, particularly with Arc melee builds. Sarpedon-D is a spread shot hand cannon which as a subfamily are rated B-Tier for endgame PvE by @TheAegisRelic tiering spreadsheet https://linktr.ee/TheAegisRelic as a huge statistical outlier but a little niche and close-range. The frame intrinsic fires a spread of 12 pellets that disappear after 25m as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. The stats are decent by comparison with its peers - you can see at https://d2foundry.gg/w/1242785638 how the weapon stats impact in-game performance. Use the barrel perk to boost Handling or tighten up the pellet spread, and then with the magazine perk go for something that helps improve the poor Reload Speed or the magazine size, although Arc builds give you access to Amplified and Spark of Frequency, and the boosts to Handling, Stability and Reload Speed. For Arc synergy, in the first trait perk column Eddy Current boosts Handling with a ×0.95 animation scalar and +20 Reload Speed with a ×0.95 animation scalar for 3s after sprinting for 1.5s, which is all applied instantly when Amplified with +60 to Reload Speed. In the second column, Voltshot applies Arc Jolt to the next target you hit within 7s (8s when enhanced), after a reload within 5.3s of a kill, for additional damage to the target on further hits plus AoE chain lightning damage on nearby enemies. You've got a choice of origin traits. You might be lucky and get the rare Accelerated Assault origin trait, which passively refills 10% of the magazine, and boosts damage by ×1.05 for getting 3 hits within a 1.6s window. Otherwise there's Problem Solver, which is very good for PvE when facing yellow bars - 4 hits from the central pellet of the spread will apply Exhaust to an enemy, reducing damage output to ×0.9 for a refreshable 5s and grant +50 to Reload Speed and Handling for 10s (12s enhanced) - both effects are also refreshable. There's also Vanguard Vindication which provides up to ×3 stacks on kills within 20s of using a class ability that reduces incoming damage to a max ×0.94 for a refreshable 10s (12.5s enhanced). Finally, Häkke Breach Armaments passively boosts damage against vehicles by ×1.15 (×1.2 enhanced), turrets and constructs by ×1.3 (×1.6 enhanced) and Stasis crystals by ×1.45. If you're interested, Sarpedon was a son of Zeus and Europa and king of Lycia, killed in the Trojan War. Recommended MW: Handling or Stability, with your preferred choice of mod to boost weapon stats, ammo generation, or elemental pickups for builds - and don't forget to check any available seasonal fragile mods.|tags:PvE PvE-Minor PvE-SingleAdd PvE-God PvE-EndGame M+KB Controller

// Oxygen SR3 - PvE Minor god AB 1 (god-pve)
// (Fluted Barrel, Polygonal Rifling, Chambered Compensator, Corkscrew Rifling), (Tactical Mag, Flared Magwell, Alloy Mag, Appended Mag), (Shoot to Loot), (One For All)
//notes:Adamsdown_Boy written as at v9.5.6.2 (PvE PvE-Minor PvE-SingleAdd PvE-God PvE-EndGame M+KB Controller): First-choice PvE roll for clearing individual Minor adds and tougher enemies in activities rated Normal, Hard or Very Hard difficulty, particularly with a Solar build. Oxygen SR3 returns still as a precision scout rifle which as a subfamily are rated D-Tier for endgame PvE by @TheAegisRelic tiering spreadsheet https://linktr.ee/TheAegisRelic as feeling bad and with a poor damage profile compared to top-tier scouts. The frame intrinsic offers a ×0.8 horizontal recoil as per the https://www.destiny2.science/ Destiny Data Compendium. The stats are decent by comparison with its peers - you can see at https://d2foundry.gg/w/4104613038 how the weapon stats impact in-game performance. You don't have to worry about Recoil Direction, so you can use the barrel perk to boost Handling or Stability, and then with the magazine perk go for something that helps improve magazine size or reload. For end-game difficulty, in the first trait perk column Shoot to Loot lets you pick up remote ammo bricks or orbs from safety by shooting them, which also reloads all your weapons from reserves. In the second, synergising with Solar builds, Burning Ambition works better to build Solar ignitions on tougher targets if you have a solar ability to apply scorch initially, otherwise it'll take 9 hits (8 when enhanced) witin 2s of each other to apply +10 scorch stacks with +10 added on each additional hit to build towards an ignition. All of the scorch stacks are increased by ×1.5 with Ember of Ashes. Ignition explosions count as weapon damage so will proc Shoot to Loot on any orbs or bricks inside the AoE. You've got a choice of origin traits. You might be lucky and get the rare Accelerated Assault origin trait, which passively refills 10% of the magazine, and boosts damage by ×1.05 for getting 3 hits within a 1.6s window. Otherwise there's Problem Solver, which is very good for PvE when facing yellow bars - 6 hits will apply Exhaust to an enemy, reducing damage output to ×0.9 for a refreshable 5s and grant +50 to Reload Speed and Handling for 10s (12s enhanced) - both effects are also refreshable. There's also Vanguard Vindication which provides up to ×3 stacks on kills within 20s of using a class ability that reduces incoming damage to a max ×0.94 for a refreshable 10s (12.5s enhanced). Finally, Omolon Fluid Dynamics boosts Stability by up to +20 and Reload Speed by up to +30 in the top half of the mag, decreasing linearly until the mag's at 50%. Recommended MW: Handling or Stability, with your preferred choice of mod to boost weapon stats, ammo generation, or elemental pickups for builds - and don't forget to check any available seasonal fragile mods.|tags:PvE PvE-Minor PvE-SingleAdd PvE-God PvE-EndGame M+KB Controller
